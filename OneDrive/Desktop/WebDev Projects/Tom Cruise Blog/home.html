Regarded as a Hollywood icon, Cruise has received an Honorary Palme d'Or, an Academy Honorary Award, and three Golden Globe Awards. As of 2025, his films have grossed more than $13.3 billion worldwide, placing him among the highest-grossing actors of all time.

Recognition

Awards & Acclaim

Cruise made his breakthrough with leading roles in Risky Business (1983) and Top Gun (1986). Critical acclaim followed with The Color of Money (1986), Rain Man (1988), and Born on the Fourth of July (1989), for which he earned a Golden Globe Award and an Academy Award for Best Actor nomination. His performance in Jerry Maguire (1996) won him a second Golden Globe for Best Actor, and Magnolia (1999) added a third Golden Globe alongside a nomination for Best Supporting Actor.

Selected Films

Cruise established himself as a star of science fiction and action films, often performing his own risky stunts. He is best known for playing fictional agent Ethan Hunt in the Mission: Impossible film series. His wider genre work spans over two decades of global blockbusters.
